# How to Set Up Telegram (5 minutes)

Your AI Chief of Staff communicates with you through Telegram. It's a free messaging app — here's how to get it.

---

## Step 1 — Download Telegram

- **iPhone:** [App Store → search "Telegram"](https://apps.apple.com/app/telegram-messenger/id686449807)
- **Android:** [Play Store → search "Telegram"](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.telegram.messenger)
- **Desktop (Mac/PC):** [telegram.org/dl](https://telegram.org/dl)

## Step 2 — Create Your Account

1. Open Telegram
2. Enter your phone number
3. Enter the verification code texted to you
4. Set your name (first name required, last name optional)

That's it. You now have Telegram.

## Step 3 — Find Your Setup Message

Once you've installed Telegram, your AI Chief of Staff will reach out to you directly. Check for a message from the bot — it will introduce itself and begin your setup walkthrough.

If you don't see a message within 5 minutes of completing your payment, email **[SUPPORT EMAIL]** and we'll connect you manually.
