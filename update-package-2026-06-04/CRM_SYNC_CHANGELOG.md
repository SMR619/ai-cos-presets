# CRM Sync Module — Changelog

## v1.0 — 2026-06-04 (Complete Module)

**What changed:**
Created module documenting automatic CRM syncing and contact management.

**Components:**
- Auto-sync to GoHighLevel (email, calls, decisions)
- Complete contact history (timeline + all interactions)
- Opportunity tracking (status + next steps)
- Contact management (organized by type/relationship)

**Key insight:**
CRM sync is "invisible" — it just happens. But having a complete history of every interaction is crucial. This module explains what you're getting and how to use it.

**Status:**
Live in presets. Agents automatically sync interactions to GHL.
