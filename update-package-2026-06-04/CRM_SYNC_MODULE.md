# CRM Sync Module

## What This Module Does

I automatically log every interaction (email, call, decision) to your CRM so you have a complete history and nothing is forgotten.

**What you get:**
- ✅ Auto-sync to GoHighLevel (every interaction, automatically)
- ✅ Complete contact history (who you've talked to, what was said, when)
- ✅ Conversation timeline (email thread + calls + notes in one place)
- ✅ Status tracking (where each opportunity stands)
- ✅ Next steps flagged (follow-ups, decisions, milestones)
- ✅ Contact management (organize and track relationships)

**Real example:**
- You send an email through Email Management
- I automatically log it to GHL with the other's contact record
- Follow-up comes in
- I log it to GHL
- Later you ask "what's the status with this person?"
- I pull their complete history from GHL

## How I Work (Agent Guidelines)

### Core Principle

Every interaction syncs automatically to your CRM (whichever you chose). You don't have to remember to log it. I do it.

**What gets logged:**
- Email sent/received
- Call summaries (if I'm on the call or you tell me)
- Decisions made
- Commitments (who owes what, by when)
- Status changes (deal moving forward, follow-up needed, etc.)

**What you see:**
- Complete contact timeline
- All history with this person
- What they want / what you committed to
- When follow-ups are due

### Example Conversations

#### **Scenario 1: "What's going on with James?"**

**You:** "Status on James from Kennedy Funding?"

**Me:** "I pulled James's contact record from GHL. Here's your history:

- May 15: Initial email to James about your lending approach
- May 16: James replies asking for proof of funds
- May 18: You send proof of funds + 2 past deals
- May 20: James requests deal package for Brownstone
- May 22: You send deal package
- **PENDING:** James should respond by May 29

Current status: Underwriting. Next step: Get term sheet from James."

---

#### **Scenario 2: "Just had a call with the seller"**

**You:** "I just talked to the seller about that Kansas City deal. He said he'd carry $150K at 6% for 10 years if we close in 60 days."

**Me:** "Got it. I'm logging this to the seller's contact record in GHL:

**Date:** Today
**Interaction:** Phone call
**Summary:** Seller agreed to carry $150K at 6% for 10 years. Condition: close within 60 days.
**Next steps:** Get title cleared, send contract

This is now in the deal record and the seller's contact history. When you ask about this seller in the future, I'll have everything right here."

---

#### **Scenario 3: "Show me my open opportunities"**

**You:** "What opportunities are pending?"

**Me:** "From GHL, you have 7 open opportunities:

1. **Kennedy Funding** — Brownstone deal in underwriting. James (contact) should respond by May 29.
2. **Valor Capital** — 3-property portfolio. Waiting on appraisal.
3. **Seller (Kansas City)** — Carry-back financing. 60-day close timeline. Title clearance pending.
4. **Investor (Texas)** — Joint venture proposal. Waiting on response to offer.
5. **Contractor (Chicago)** — Repair quote. Due by May 25.
6. **Realtor** — Wholesale deal referral. Showing property May 22.
7. **Attorney** — LLC formation. Documents in progress.

**Needs attention:**
- Contractor quote is due May 25 (3 days)
- Kennedy Funding response due May 29 (7 days)

Want me to flag these for follow-up?"
