# Your AI Chief of Staff Preset Package

Welcome to your complete AI Chief of Staff system.

This preset includes everything your AI agent needs to be smart, personalized, and autonomous.

---

## What's Included

### 🏗️ Foundation Systems (The Brain)

**5 Setup Guides + Pre-Built Infrastructure:**
1. **Memory Architecture** — How your agent remembers you and your business
2. **Operating Rules** — Your decision-making framework  
3. **Frameworks Library** — Proven thinking patterns and communication models
4. **Tools Knowledge Base** — 100+ vetted tools searchable by problem
5. **Knowledge Base System** — How your agent learns and improves

**Supporting Folders:**
- `frameworks/` — Complete framework library (communication, business, operations, financial, technical)
- `knowledge/` — Structured folders for storing research and lessons (outputs/, lessons/)
- `wiki/` — Template for your domain knowledge (businesses, deals, people, rules, procedures)

### 📋 Customer Modules (What You Do)

**8 complete, ready-to-use modules:**
1. **How Your Agent Works** (Foundation) — Explains everything
2. **Email Management** — Draft, send, track emails intelligently
3. **Deal Tracker** — Track deals, analyze quality, calculate ROI
4. **CRM Sync** — Manage contacts, sync interactions across platforms
5. **Content Creation & Brand Journal** — Mine wins, draft content, build brand
6. **Outreach Support** — Build contact lists, draft emails, schedule follow-ups
7. **Research & Analysis** — Analyze videos, articles, links for insights
8. **Weekly Level-Up** — Review your week, identify blind spots, improve

Each module includes:
- Setup checklist (realistic, 5-30 min)
- Agent guidelines (how it works)
- Example conversations
- Foundation references (what it depends on)
- Changelog (version history)
