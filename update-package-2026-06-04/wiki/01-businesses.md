# Your Businesses

Document each of your businesses here. Your agent uses this to understand what you do and how you operate.

## Format

For each business, include:
- What it does
- Revenue model
- Key metrics
- Current status
- Recent wins
- Current challenges

## Template

```markdown
## [Business Name]

**What it does:** [One-sentence description of what the business does]

**Revenue model:** [How the business makes money]

**Team:** [Key people involved, their roles]

**Key metrics:** [What success looks like - revenue targets, customer count, conversion rate, etc.]

**Status:** [Active / Growth mode / Maintenance / Scaling / Other]

**Recent wins:** [What's working well, what's driving growth]

**Current challenges:** [What's not working, what needs fixing]

**Links:** [Any relevant files, documents, dashboards, or resources]
```

## Add Your Businesses Below

Add one section per business using the format above.

Start with your main/largest business, then add others.

Your agent will read these to understand your portfolio, your priorities, and how to help each business succeed.
