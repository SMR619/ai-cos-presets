# People & Relationships

Document key people in your network: partners, investors, team members, vendors, contacts, etc.

Your agent uses this to communicate appropriately when handling relationships on your behalf.

## Format

For each important person, include:
- Who they are / what they do
- Relationship to you
- Important context
- How to work with them
- Communication preferences

## Template

```markdown
## [Person Name]

**Title/Role:** [Their position or what they do]

**Relationship:** [How you know them - partner, investor, team member, vendor, contact, etc.]

**What they do:** [Their business or profession]

**Important context:** [Key facts your agent should know - their goals, constraints, what matters to them]

**How to work with them:** [Their communication style, preferences, what they respond to]

**Recent interactions:** [Last time you communicated, what you discussed, any open items]

**Communication preferences:** [Email vs phone, time zones, frequency they like to hear from you]

**Links:** [Their website, LinkedIn, contact info, deal files involving them, etc.]
```

---

## Tips

- **Be specific about communication style** — Does this person prefer email or calls? Quick notes or detailed analysis? Formal or casual?
- **Note their priorities** — What matters to them? What are they trying to accomplish?
- **Document history** — Previous deals, interactions, lessons learned from working together
- **Include constraints** — Time zones, availability, industry-specific requirements
