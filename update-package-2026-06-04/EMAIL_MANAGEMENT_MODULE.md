# Email Management Module

## What This Module Does

I manage your email so nothing important falls through the cracks and nothing takes forever to write.

**What you get:**
- ✅ Draft emails using frameworks (Communication Playbook + proven persuasion techniques)
- ✅ Review emails before sending (for accuracy, tone, impact)
- ✅ Send emails through the right account (MRM, Level 19, PMB, or personal)
- ✅ Track email interactions (logs to CRM automatically)
- ✅ Understand context (because I remember your business, relationships, goals)

**What you don't get:**
- Inbox scanning automation (you ask me to review, I do)
- Auto-sorting (you control what I do with email)

**Real example:**
- You: "Draft an email to this lender about the deal"
- Me: I remember the lender's profile, your relationship, deal specifics, and what you need
- Me: I draft using frameworks (what worked before, proven structure)
- Me: I send through the right email account with correct signature
- You: Review, approve, or edit before it goes out

## What You Need (Setup Checklist)

### Step 1: Email Account Setup (10-15 minutes)

You have two options: Business email (Microsoft 365) or Personal email.

**Option A: Business Email (Recommended)**

If you have a Microsoft 365 account for your business:

1. **Get your Microsoft Graph credentials:**
   - Go to: https://portal.azure.com
   - Create an app registration (or ask your IT person)
   - You'll get: Tenant ID, Client ID, Client Secret

2. **Tell me your email address:**
   - Example: ava@yourbusiness.com
   - This is what I'll send from

3. **Store credentials:**
   - I'll store these in your `credentials.env` file
   - Command: Ask me to "Set up Microsoft email with these credentials"

4. **Test it:**
   - Ask me: "Send a test email to myself"
   - I'll verify it works

**Option B: Personal Email (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, etc.)**

If you want to use your personal email:

1. **Generate an app password** (more secure than regular password):
   - Gmail: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
   - Outlook: https://account.microsoft.com/security
   - Yahoo: https://login.yahoo.com

2. **Tell me your email address:**
   - Example: your@gmail.com
   - This is what I'll send from

3. **Provide app password:**
   - Ask me: "Set up email with [your-email] and app password [xxxxx]"
   - I'll store it securely

4. **Test it:**
   - Ask me: "Send a test email to myself"
   - I'll verify it works

**That's it.** Once verified, ready to go.

### Step 2: Email Signature Setup (2 minutes)

Your email signature auto-appends. It's styled correctly for each account:
- L19: "Ava Langley / Executive Assistant / Level 19 Homes / Ava@Level19Homes.com / www.Level19Homes.com"

- PMB: "Ava Langley / Executive Assistant / Political Media Buyers / Ava@PoliticalMediaBuyers.com / www.PoliticalMediaBuyers.com"

No setup needed. I handle it.

### Step 3: Ready to Use

That's it. You can now:
- Ask me to draft emails
- Ask me to send emails
- Ask me to review a response
- Track interactions

## How I Work (Agent Guidelines)

### Core Principle

When you ask me to draft or send an email, I:
1. **Remember context** — your business, this person, your relationship, what's at stake
2. **Apply frameworks** — Communication Playbook (PAS framework, etc.), Persuasion, Objection Handling
3. **Use your voice** — matches how you talk, not generic corporate
4. **Verify recipients** — before sending, I triple-check you mean THIS person, not someone else
5. **Auto-signature** — correct account, correct signature, no mistakes

### Example Conversations

#### **Scenario 1: "Draft an email to this lender"**

**You:** "Draft an email to James at Kennedy Funding. I need to request a term sheet for the Brownstone deal."

[Draft email follows, showing:]
- Problem (what they help with)
- Why this deal fits
- What you need
- Next step

Before I send it, you review and approve. Once you say go, I send it from your L19 email with the correct signature."
