### 11. Neuroscience of Addictive Storytelling (4-Step Loop)
**Source:** YouTube — storytelling creator 1M+ followers (2026-05-30)  
**Applications:** cold_outreach, content_creation, marketing, objection_handling, negotiation, relationship_building

Dopamine is the **prediction chemical** (not pleasure). Fires during anticipation, not reward. The 4-step loop:
1. **Stakes** — establish why it matters; context that activates the brain
2. **Big Question** — load a question the brain must answer → sustained dopamine drip
3. **Head Fake** — break the prediction (surprise = largest dopamine spike)
4. **Payoff** — resolve tension, then immediately reload with new question

Chain the loops = addiction. Slot machines beat vending machines because of uncertainty, not rewards.

**Integration:** Layer onto SB7 (each SB7 stage gets a loaded question), Internal Tension (Big Question IS the NEPQ gap), Problem-Solution-Reality (Stakes = Problem framing).

### 12. CIA Targeting Principle
**Source:** Former CIA officer interview, YouTube (2026-05-30)  
**Applications:** cold_outreach, marketing, content_creation, negotiation, relationship_building

**Core rule:** Never try to persuade against existing beliefs. Find people who already believe 60-90% of what you need, then push.

Russia/Iran influence operations: Found existing divisions, funded both sides, amplified what was already there — never tried to change minds.

**Steps:** (1) Define the belief your offer requires → (2) Find audiences already holding 60-90% of that belief → (3) Confirm and amplify their existing direction → (4) Your message is permission, not argument.

**Integration:** NEPQ (amplify gap they already feel), Tactical Empathy (label what they already believe), BCSM (only works if they're already on the stairway).

### 13. LAPS Sales System (10-Point Meeting)
**Source:** 25-year sales veteran, YouTube (2026-05-30)  
**Applications:** cold_outreach, negotiation, objection_handling, relationship_building

**LAPS = Leads → Appointments → Presentations → Sales**

**Content Rule:** People notice you on the 11th time they see you. Clock resets every 90 days. Post every day or accept invisibility.

**10-Point Meeting:** (1) Set agenda → (2) Rapport (2-3 min) → (3) Discovery → (4) Dream outcome → (5) Current pain → (6) **Cost of inaction** (most missed) → (7) Solution mapped to THEIR pain → (8) Objections with empathy → (9) Clear next step → (10) Follow-up system (5 touches / 5 weeks)

**Integration:** Steps 3-6 = NEPQ Internal Tension. Steps 2,8 = Tactical Empathy. Step 7 = SB7. CIA Targeting applies pre-meeting.

### 14. SPCL Influence Building (Hormozi)
**Source:** Alex Hormozi YouTube Live (2026-05-30)  
**Applications:** content_creation, marketing, relationship_building, cold_outreach

**S = Status** — Controls scarce resource audience wants (context-dependent)  
**P = Proof** — Evidence status is real (numbers, screenshots, case studies, track record)  
**C = Content** — Volume + consistency. 35K pieces/year. Builds compound influence not just views.  
**L = Leverage** — Each piece leverages existing status and proof

You don't need to be creative. You need work ethic + this framework.

### 15. Proof Story → IP Monetization (Priestley)
**Source:** Daniel Priestley, YouTube (2026-05-30)  
**Applications:** marketing, content_creation, cold_outreach, relationship_building

Proof story = special thing you did (ICP-relevant) + quantifiable named outcome + step-by-step process.

**5-Step:** (1) Find proof story → (2) Name and own the IP → (3) Two products: Prospects (learn) + Core Offering (delivered) → (4) Infrastructure: landing page + slide deck → (5) Key Person of Influence in niche

**Integration:** SB7 (proof story = guide's backstory), CIA Targeting (targets people already believing in the problem), LAPS Step 7.

### 16. Herd Effect Launch
**Source:** Dan Henry, YouTube (2026-05-30) — $47K in one hour  
**Applications:** marketing, content_creation, cold_outreach

Can't engineer herd effect one-on-one. Need many people in one space simultaneously.

**4-Step:** (1) Free community → (2) One-to-many presentation (webinar/live) → (3) Visible social proof cascade during purchase → (4) Paid community behind gate (buyers see each other → FOMO for fence-sitters)

**The sauce most miss:** Ask buyers to publicly post in free community. Fence-sitters see the herd moving and buy.
