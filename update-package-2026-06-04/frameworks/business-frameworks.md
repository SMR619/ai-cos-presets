## Frameworks Added 2026-05-30 (from Video Analysis)

### Billion-Dollar Niche Extraction
**Source:** Starter Story — David/Shipper interview (2026-05-30)  
**Bootstrapped SaaS from 0 to $50K MRR by "copying" a billion-dollar company narrowly**

1. Find a billion-dollar company with massive traction
2. List their 3 most common user pain points
3. Build narrowly focused solution for #1 pain point ONLY
4. Price 30-50% below big player
5. Win on specificity and support quality

### Asymmetric Advantage Loop
**Source:** CEO/investor YouTube (2026-05-30)  
**Two forces must align: Your asymmetric advantage ↔ Customer's acute pain**

- Asymmetric advantage = experience/access/knowledge nobody else has
- Acute pain = specific frustration that is urgent + frequent + painful
- Win when these two align AND each win accelerates the next

Investment banker example: 20 years of deal knowledge → laid off → bought insurance claims company → infused AI → compounded.

### CHAOS Framework (Business Testing)
**Source:** Daniel Priestley (2026-05-30)  
**For testing new business ideas before scaling**

CHAOS = Concept + Audience + Offer + Sales
- Run as 90-day open-and-shut experiments
- 2-person scout team (mentor/co-founder/assistant)
- MVPs = slide decks, brochures, pre-sales (not full products)
- Don't scale until all 4 elements proven

### Funding Choice Framework
**Source:** Startup funding analysis YouTube (2026-05-30)  
**Decision gate: Do you need 10-300x scale?**

- VC money = commitment to rapid exit (acquisition or IPO). Founders lose control.
- Bootstrap = ownership + control + durable profitability
- 2026 context: AI removed most early-stage arguments FOR taking VC money
- Travis Kalanick (Uber): <9% by IPO. Stuart Butterfield (Slack): ~8% at IPO.

### Paywall Conversion Stack
**Source:** RevenueCat 100 Paywalls Study (2026-05-30)  
**For any subscription product pricing page**

Layer 1: Deliver aha moment BEFORE paywall (within first session)  
Layer 2: Social proof at paywall moment  
Layer 3: Risk reversal (money-back guarantee)  
Layer 4: Annual anchor first → monthly looks cheap  
Layer 5: Personalization ("Based on your goals...")  
Layer 6: 3-tier pricing (Good/Better/Best — middle = target)  

Hard paywalls convert 5x better than freemium. 55% of cancellations happen on Day Zero.
